How to Use These Templates

  1. Click "Use This Template" to create your own copy
  2. Fill in the Metadata sheet with your information
  3. Add your vocabulary data to the Input sheet
  4. Click "OHDSI Validation > Validate Template" from the menu
  5. Review results in the Output sheet and fix any errors
  6. When validation passes, click "Submit Final Version"
